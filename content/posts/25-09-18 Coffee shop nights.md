Not too long before, once per week, I started going out to sit at a particular coffee shop after work. There I would just open my sketch book and go ham on what ever thing was on my mind that time, or what I wanted to draw at that time. I found out I really like to mood. My mind is more active compared to when I stayed at home.

So I increase that to 2 days per week. It's at a great ratio right now. I get to let out some creativity every week. Going to keep this up.
