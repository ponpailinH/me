# Hit goes boom

1. I want the game to be more "stylized", maybe blocky? maybe pixel art? maybe lowpoly? but definitely not realistic in terms of tank designs. So I changed the turret & gun model.

2. I added the impact explosion. It could be better, but this is quite cool for a quickly-made effect. One thing I already don't like about it is that the glow seems to *darken*, instead of gradienting into transparent color.
