# Next-step

I'm planning to start a new scene/unity project to do a **tech study** of RTS unit navigation system. Precisely, similar to [***Beyond All Reason***](https://www.beyondallreason.info)'s navigation system; where human (bot) units have free 360 degree movement, but vehicle units have to move either forward or backwards, they actually have to "turn" to move forward. Tracked vehicles may have 0 turn radius, but wheeled vehicles will have more than 0.

![Beyond All Reason](<https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/5c68622246b367adf6f3041d/62cbd19b33c6502672cf5262_BAR_scale-realism2web.jpg>)

Certain units can climb steep hills, or go into forests, while others cannot.

There is a vision / line-of-sight system.

And many more! I'm sooooooooo excitedddddddd.
